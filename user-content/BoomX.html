Signals for pivots include:
Gray Triangle: Pivot (Any pivot that is not listed below)
Yellow Triangle: First Pullback (change from LH to HL or HL to LH)
Teal triangle: Higher Low (HL)
Red Triangle: Lower High (LH)
Orange Triangle: Reversal (HH or LL near the top or bottom of the channel)
