Center Of Gravity XL

Introducing the most powerful Center Of Gravity indicator yet. Loaded with 2 oscillators COG XL will help you find all kinds of entries and avoid bad ones. Both Oscillators are packed full of alerts.

How to use

Each line on both oscillators serves a purpose and has a unique strategy.

Oscillator 1

Oscillator 1 is developed to show momentum and trend. It is the perfect companion for Boom hunter XL, it shows the things that XL does not, like how far price action is from its median. It is also good for tracking channels and finding entries like pullback entries to help you bet with the trend. This setup also includes two customizable MA lines with 11 choices including two of my own (ZLSMA and WMA MOD).

Main oscillator (White line)

The white line is the main oscillator used to enter trades, find chart reversal patterns, and isolate breakouts. The price line changes color with pressure in red or green. 

Pressure (Red or Green shadow)

Showing pressure and how far it is from its center point. The more solid the color the stronger shows momentum and trend.

Moving Average 1 (Yellow line)

Customizable Moving Average (MA) setting (SMA, SMMA, EMA, LSMA, ZLSMA, HULL, WMA, VWMA, WMA MOD,  ALMA, VWAP)

Moving Average 2 (Blue line)

Customizable second MA setting

 

Best to be use along with Boom hunter XL to track channel, trend, and momentum

Oscillator 2

Oscillator 2 is the traditional COG setup but with pressure indicator. COG is the best indicator for reading charts, it uses LSMA as a zero line. It will show on going levels which indicate the trend. It is a good complement to Boom hunter XL who shows support and resistance (SR) within a trend. In other terms it highlights bottoms and tops. It is great for scalping or finding first pullbacks entries.

Main oscillator (Blue line)

The blue line represent the main oscillator, it also includes pressure analysis indicated as changing red or green, that use the same concept as Boom Hunter XL.

Moving Average (Red line)

LSMA 200 as a dynamic zero line median.

Multiple Center of Gravity (CoG) can be used to detect different pivots of the chart for more confluence.
