Q: What is purpose of the long shadow?

A: it shows the previous high and low. The best thing about this indicator is the ability to show support and resistance lines. For an example, if it cant break previous high there is a good chance there will be a good short.

Q: Do you have a preference (lately) of CoG or Boom Pro? Boom features an adaptation of CoG, right? I've been comparing them today, but can't decide - there were some different signals at times.

A: I use both CoG and Boom Pro for different things. Boom will show support and resistance level within a trend but no further than that. CoG will show on going levels. In this screenshot you can see how at each boom it has recorded a new low point on the CoG. Each low does not break the previous low which indicates an up trend.
