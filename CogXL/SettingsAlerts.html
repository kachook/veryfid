Variations of settings for Moving averages:

  • ZLSMA 200 and HULL 25
  • ZLSMA 200 and ALMA 32
  • ZLSMA 200 and LSMA 50
  • ZLSMA 25, on the 45m time frame
  • WMA MOD 200, on the 1h time frame
  • ZLSMA 200 and HULL 200, faster time frames
  • ZLSMA 50, daily time frame
  • LSMA 155 and VWMA 25

Try playing around with to see what you like, there is no wrong answers.
